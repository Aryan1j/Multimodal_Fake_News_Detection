ABOUT YOUR DATASET
==================

This directory should contain your WELFake-style dataset CSV file.

Required file: WELFake_Dataset.csv

Required columns:
- title: Article headline (string)
- text: Article body text (string)
- label: Binary classification (0 = Real, 1 = Fake)

Where to get the dataset:
- Kaggle: Search for "WELFake Dataset"
- Zenodo: WELFake benchmark dataset
- Any CSV following the same column format

Using a custom dataset path:
  python -m src.train --data /path/to/your/dataset.csv

Handling tiny datasets:
- Very small datasets may produce unstable metrics
- Minimum recommended: 1000+ samples
- For quick testing, use train_quick.py with --max-samples

Split mismatch errors:
If you change the dataset or cleaning flags, delete results/data_split.json
and retrain to regenerate splits.
